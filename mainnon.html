Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that combines marinated chicken with basmati rice, aromatic spices, and herbs. The chicken is marinated in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is layered with partially cooked rice, fried onions, mint, and saffron, then slow-cooked in a sealed pot to allow the flavors to meld. The result is tender chicken infused with the rich flavors of the spices, complemented by the aromatic rice. Chicken Biryani is traditionally served with raita and boiled eggs.

Mutton Rogan Josh is a signature dish from Kashmir, known for its deep red color and rich flavor. Tender pieces of mutton are slow-cooked in a gravy made from yogurt, onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices, including Kashmiri red chili, cardamom, and cinnamon. The slow cooking allows the mutton to absorb the spices, resulting in a dish that is both flavorful and aromatic. Mutton Rogan Josh is typically served with steamed rice or naan, making it a hearty and satisfying main course that is deeply loved across India.

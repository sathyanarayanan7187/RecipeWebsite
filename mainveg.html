VEG MAINCOURSE

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy and mildly spiced North Indian dish. Cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) are simmered in a rich tomato-based gravy made with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices like garam masala, cumin, and kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves). The gravy is smooth, slightly sweet, and tangy, perfectly complementing the soft paneer. This dish is typically served with naan, roti, or steamed rice, making it a popular choice for vegetarians. The creamy texture and rich flavors make Paneer Butter Masala a comforting and satisfying main course.

Baingan Bharta is a smoky and flavorful dish made from roasted eggplants. The eggplants are roasted until soft, then mashed and cooked with onions, tomatoes, garlic, green chilies, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The roasting process gives the eggplant a deep, smoky flavor that pairs beautifully with the spiced tomato-onion base. Baingan Bharta is usually served with roti or paratha and is a great way to enjoy the earthy taste of eggplant. This dish is loved for its unique texture and robust flavors.

Chole, also known as Chana Masala, is a hearty and spicy North Indian dish made with chickpeas. The chickpeas are cooked in a thick gravy made from onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and a special blend of spices including cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The dish is typically garnished with fresh cilantro and served with bhature (deep-fried bread) or rice. Chole has a rich and tangy flavor, with the chickpeas absorbing the spices and gravy, making it a satisfying and protein-rich main course. Aloo Gobi is a simple yet delicious vegetarian dish made with potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi). The vegetables are cooked together with onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, and garam masala. The turmeric gives the dish its vibrant yellow color, while the spices add warmth and depth to the flavor. Aloo Gobi is typically served with roti or paratha, making it a comforting and wholesome meal. Its balance of textures, with the soft potatoes and tender cauliflower, makes it a beloved dish in Indian households.

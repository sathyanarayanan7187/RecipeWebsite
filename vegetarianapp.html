VEGETARIAN APPETIZERS

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes. To prepare, mix yogurt with spices like red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and chaat masala. Add ginger-garlic paste and lemon juice. Coat paneer cubes in this mixture and let them marinate for at least an hour. Skewer the paneer with bell peppers and onions. Grill or bake at 200¬∞C (392¬∞F) for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally until charred. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Aloo Tikki is a crispy potato patty often served with chutneys. Boil and mash potatoes, then mix in breadcrumbs, chopped green chilies, coriander, and spices like cumin powder, garam masala, and salt. Form the mixture into patties. Shallow fry the tikkis on medium heat in oil until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with tamarind and mint chutneys, garnished with chopped onions, coriander, and a sprinkle of chaat masala.

Vegetable Samosas are deep-fried pastry pockets filled with spiced vegetables. To make the filling, saut√© cumin seeds in oil, add chopped onions, ginger, garlic, green chilies, and boiled mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots). Season with spices like turmeric, coriander, garam masala, and salt. Cool the mixture, then stuff into rolled dough triangles. Seal the edges and deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot with tamarind chutney or green chutney.

Hara Bhara Kabab is a healthy and delicious green vegetable patty. Blanch spinach and peas, then blend with boiled potatoes, green chilies, ginger, and spices like cumin powder, garam masala, and salt. Add breadcrumbs for binding and shape into patties. Shallow fry on medium heat until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon slices, garnished with chaat masala.
